Casa Rosa Mutante

Casa Rosa Mutante has since its beginning been a space for openly investigating production and distribution processes regarding art, all with the aim of cultivating critical audiences capable of acknowledging cultural and artistic efforts in Oaxaca, from public policies to independent art projects, from the perspective of those of us working to spread and promote artistic practices within the community.

Espacio Lalitho

Espacio Lalitho is a platform for the production and dissemination of contemporary art, allowing for research and for the presentation of national and international projects within Oaxaca City’s supply market. It aims for a collective practice that is in dialogue with and integrated into that context. It has participated in local and national exhibitions of contemporary art, and is currently a recipient of the PAC grant.
